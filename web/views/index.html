About Me

I'm an 18-year-old male, who's a permanent resident of Folsom, California. However, I am temporarily living in West Lafayette, Indiana on a seasonal basis to attend Purdue University.

I'm current a student at Purdue University pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and Data Science. I expect to graduate 2023. I also work as a part-time Robot Operator/Field Assistant at Starship Technologies. In my (little) spare time outside of work and studies I like to code, occasionally play video games, watch movies, tinker with mechanical keyboards and geek out over the latest tech.

I love low-level programming, and thus strongly prefer using C++, C, and D in my projects. I'm currently learning Python as a supplemental scripting language. I also have experience with other programming languages, but do not prefer them, and am not nearly as proficient in them as I am in C/C++/D.

I primarily use Linux on all of my computers and servers, but have a good amount of Windows WinAPI programming experience. I have collected 2 physical rack-mount servers, and set them up in my dorm mostly for fun. They serve very little practical purpose other than giving me experience in (decade old) enterprise hardware, and a sandbox to play with.
